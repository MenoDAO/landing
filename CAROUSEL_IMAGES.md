# Carousel Images Setup

## Where to Place Images

Place your carousel images in: **`/public/carousel/`**

## Naming Convention

Name your images exactly as:
- `slide-1.jpg` (or `.png`, `.webp`)
- `slide-2.jpg`
- `slide-3.jpg`

## Image Specifications

**Recommended settings:**
- **Format**: JPG (best for photos), PNG (if you need transparency), or WebP
- **Aspect Ratio**: 16:9 or 21:9 (wide format works best for carousel)
- **Resolution**: At least 1920x1080px (Full HD) or higher for crisp display
- **File Size**: Optimize images to under 500KB each for faster loading

## Image Tips

1. **High Quality**: Use vibrant, high-contrast images that represent your brand
2. **Center Focus**: Important elements should be centered (text overlays the images)
3. **Content Match**: 
   - Slide 1: Community, healthcare, people together
   - Slide 2: Affordable, accessible healthcare scenes
   - Slide 3: Technology, transparency, trust-building imagery
4. **Legibility**: The carousel has dark overlays and text shadows, so any image will work - the text will remain legible

## Fallback

If images are missing, the carousel will automatically show beautiful gradient backgrounds, so your site will work perfectly even before adding images.

## Swipe Navigation

The carousel supports:
- **Touch/Swipe**: Swipe left/right on mobile devices to navigate
- **Click Indicators**: Click the dots at the bottom to jump to any slide
- **Auto-play**: Automatically advances every 5 seconds

No navigation arrows are shown - the carousel is designed for touch-friendly interaction!
